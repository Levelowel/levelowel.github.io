### Question 1: I'm having problems with mods, what should I do?
  Answer: Some mods mess with shaders, but there are ways to fix them. Check out the [known mod issue fixes](https://justpaste.it/60cgu).

### Question 2: It says the resource pack is made for a newer Minecraft version, what should I do?
  Answer: If you are playing on 1.13 or a newer version, just ignore that message and keep on going. There won't be any problems. But if you are playing on 1.12 or a lower version, you can't use the resource pack. Please enable the Compatibility Mode in "Shader Options" -> "Profile" on the top left.
  
### Question 3: The sky looks wrong, what should I do?
  Answer: Go to "Options" -> "Video Settings" -> "Details" and enable the "Sky".
  
### Question 4: Water and/or stained glass looks wrong, what should I do?
  Answer: That's a bug caused by old graphics drivers. All you need to do to fix the issue is updating your graphics drivers.
